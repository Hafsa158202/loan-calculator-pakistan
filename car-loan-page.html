Car Loan in Pakistan

A car loan in Pakistan is a financing option offered by banks and Islamic financial institutions that helps individuals purchase a new or used vehicle by paying in monthly installments instead of a full upfront payment. The bank covers the majority of the carâ€™s price, while the borrower returns the amount over time, along with either interest or, in the case of Islamic financing, a halal profit structure such as Murabaha or Diminishing Musharakah. Car loans in Pakistan are popular because they make owning a vehicle more affordable, especially for salaried and self-employed individuals who may not have large savings available at once. With growing demand, most banks now provide flexible repayment periods, down-payment options, and fast approval processes, making car financing a convenient gateway to car ownership for many Pakistanis.
