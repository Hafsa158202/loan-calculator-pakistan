Flat vs Reducing Loan in Pakistan

When it comes to personal, business, or car financing in Pakistan, most people hear two terms repeatedly: Flat Rate Loan and Reducing Balance Loan. Both sound similar, but the way they charge profit/interest is completely different â€” and this difference can change your monthly installment and total repayment drastically. Understanding this concept helps you choose the right financing model and avoid paying extra. In simple words, a flat loan keeps your monthly installments almost constant. In contrast, a reducing loan lowers your installment over time because the profit is charged only on the remaining principal amount. Pakistani banks, Islamic financing programs, and microfinance institutions use both models depending on the product and customer type.

Flat Interest

  • Interest on full loan amount throughout tenure.
  • EMI remains constant.
  • Total payable amount is higher.
