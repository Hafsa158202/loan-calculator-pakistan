What is Ijara?

Ijara is a contract where the bank purchases an asset and leases it to the customer for a fixed period. Ownership remains with the bank, while the customer enjoys the right to use the asset in return for periodic rentals.

  • Commonly used for home and car financing
  • Ownership stays with the bank, usage with the customer
  • Shariah-compliant alternative to interest-based leasing
