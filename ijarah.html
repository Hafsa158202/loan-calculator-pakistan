Ijara (Islamic Leasing)

A Shariah-Compliant Alternative to Conventional Leasing

üè¶ What is Ijara?

Ijara is an Islamic financing concept where the bank purchases an asset (like a car, home, or equipment) and leases it to the customer for a fixed period. Ownership remains with the bank, while the customer enjoys the right to use the asset in exchange for periodic rental payments.

  • Used for car, home, or equipment financing
  • Ownership stays with the bank, usage with the customer
  • Shariah-compliant alternative to interest-based leasing

üìÑ How Does Ijara Work?

  1. The customer selects the asset they wish to lease (e.g., a car or home).
  2. The bank purchases the asset and retains ownership.
  3. The customer signs a lease agreement for a fixed period and rental amount.
  4. Customer makes regular rental payments (not interest-based).
  5. At the end of the lease, the bank may transfer ownership to the customer (Ijara-wa-Iqtina model).
