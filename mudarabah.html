Mudarabah is a partnership where one party provides the capital (Rab-ul-Maal) and the other provides management and expertise (Mudarib). Profits are shared based on a pre-agreed ratio, while losses are borne only by the capital provider.

  • Used in deposit schemes and Islamic investments
  • Profit ratios are agreed upon in advance
  • Shariah-compliant alternative to interest-based financing
