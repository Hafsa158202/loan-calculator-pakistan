Ijara (Islamic Leasing)

A Shariah-Compliant Alternative to Conventional Leasing in Pakistan

üè¶ What is Ijara?

Ijara is an Islamic financing structure where the bank purchases an asset and leases it to the customer for a fixed period. The ownership remains with the bank while the customer pays agreed-upon rentals. This method avoids any form of interest (riba) and ensures the transaction is fully Shariah-compliant.

  • Used for car, home, and equipment financing
  • Ownership remains with the bank, usage with the customer
  • Interest-free and transparent rental model

üìÑ How Does Ijara Work?

  1. The customer selects the asset they wish to lease.
  2. The bank purchases the asset and retains ownership.
  3. A lease agreement is signed for a fixed period and rent.
  4. The customer pays regular rentals (not interest-based).
  5. At lease-end, ownership may transfer to the customer (Ijara-wa-Iqtina model).
