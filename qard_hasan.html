What is Qard Hasan?

Qard Hasan is an Islamic interest-free loan offered to individuals or businesses in need, without expecting any return or profit. It is usually provided to support social welfare, education, healthcare, or small business initiatives.

  • Promotes social welfare and community support
  • Completely interest-free financial help
  • Reflects the social responsibility of Islamic banks
