Istisna (Manufacturing Contract)

An Islamic Mode of Construction & Project Financing

What is Istisna?

Istisna is a contract where a buyer places an order for the manufacturing or construction of an asset. The price and delivery date are agreed in advance. It is widely used in housing finance and industrial project development.

  • Used in housing and industrial projects
  • Price and delivery time are fixed in advance
  • Based on future delivery agreements
