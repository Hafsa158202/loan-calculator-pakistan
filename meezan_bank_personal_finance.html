🏦 Introduction

Meezan Bank, Pakistan’s first and largest Islamic bank, offers personal finance solutions that align with Shariah principles, ensuring transparency and ethical banking. Whether you’re managing education expenses, emergencies, or personal needs, Meezan Bank provides financial support without compromising Islamic values.

Meezan Bank follows the principles of Islamic finance, avoiding interest (Riba) and ensuring all transactions are transparent, fair, and backed by real assets. Financing is offered through Shariah-approved modes like Murabaha and Musawamah.

  • Shariah Board approved contracts
  • Flexible monthly repayment options
