Wadiah (Safekeeping Account)

An Interest-Free Islamic Account System

What is Wadiah?

Wadiah is an Islamic safekeeping system in which a customer deposits their money with the bank for safekeeping. The bank guarantees the security of funds but does not pay any interest. It is commonly used for current accounts.

  • Widely used in current accounts
  • Completely free from interest (Riba)
  • Secure Islamic method of depositing money
