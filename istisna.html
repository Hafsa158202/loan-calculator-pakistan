An Islamic Mode of Construction & Project Financing

Istisna is an Islamic contract where a buyer places an order for the manufacturing or construction of an asset. The price and delivery date are agreed upon in advance. It is commonly used in housing and industrial project financing.

  • Used in housing and industrial project development
  • Price and delivery time are pre-agreed
  • Based on future delivery agreements (not interest-based)
